Lopez Beef Tapa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Beef tapa (any kind)
  • Mushrooms
  • 1/2 knorr beef cube
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Butter or margarine
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Garlic

Step-by-Step Procedure:

  1. Make fire in the stove. And get your frying pan and put it in the stove, then wait until it's hot.
  2. For toasted garlic: Chop the garlic. Heat oil in a pan and place the sliced garlic. Stir until lightly golden brown and set aside.
  3. Place the beef tapa in a hot pan.
  4. Add water and boil until evaporates.
  5. Add oil and fry the beef tapa until cooked. Set aside (make sure the beef looks brownish when done).
  6. For the gravy: melt butter or margarine in a pan and mix in the flour.
  7. Add 1 cup of water and stir to combine.
  8. Add the beef cube, pepper, and soy sauce. Mix and simmer for 1 minute.
  9. Add the mushrooms and the cooked beef tapa. Mix and simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  10. Add the toasted garlic. Mix and it's done!.
